RAID Simulator II

Explore advanced RAID levels: 4, 5, 6, and 10 with dedicated/rotating parity, double parity, and mirrored striping.

Key Concepts

RAID 4 (Block-level Parity)
Block-level striping with a dedicated parity disk.
RAID 5 (Distributed Parity)
Parity rotates across disks, balancing write pressure.
RAID 6 (Double Parity)
Two parity blocks per stripe; survives two disk failures.
RAID 10 (Mirrored Stripe)
Striping across mirrored pairs for high performance and redundancy.
