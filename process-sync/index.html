Key Concepts

Race Condition
A situation where multiple processes access and manipulate shared data, and the outcome depends on the order of execution.
Critical Section
A segment of code where shared resources are accessed. Only one process should be in its critical section at a time.
Semaphore
An integer variable accessed through two standard atomic operations: wait() and signal(). Used for signaling among processes.
Mutex Lock
A strict locking mechanism used to guarantee mutual exclusion to a shared resource.
Deadlock
A situation where two or more processes are waiting indefinitely for an event that can only be caused by one of the waiting processes.
Starvation
A situation where a process is perpetually denied the resources it needs to process its work.
