Disk Scheduling

Animate head movement for common disk scheduling algorithms and compare seek behavior.

Key Concepts

Seek Time
Time taken to move the disk head to the target cylinder.
FCFS
First-Come, First-Served. Simplest, but typically yields high seek times.
SSTF
Shortest Seek Time First. Closer requests served first. May cause starvation.
SCAN
Elevator algorithm. Sweeps end-to-end.
C-SCAN
Circular SCAN. Only services requests while moving in one direction.
LOOK / C-LOOK
Variants of SCAN/C-SCAN that reverse immediately after the last request instead of hitting the disk bounds.
