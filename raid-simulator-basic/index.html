RAID Simulator I

Explore foundational RAID levels: 0, 1, 2, and 3 with striping, mirroring, ECC, and dedicated parity behavior.

Key Concepts

RAID 0 (Striping)
Distributes data across disks for high performance but no redundancy.
RAID 1 (Mirroring)
Duplicates data across disks for high redundancy.
RAID 2 (Bit-level ECC)
Bit-level striping with ECC disks for correction.
RAID 3 (Byte-level Parity)
Byte-level striping with one dedicated parity disk.
